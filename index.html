How it works

Three simple steps to a cleaner cloud.

1

Connect your providers

Sign in with Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive. CirroGuide uses official OAuth — your password is never shared with us.

2

Scan your storage

CirroGuide reads file metadata and creates content hashes to build your storage summary and identify duplicates.

3

Review and clean up

Preview duplicates, choose what to keep, and reclaim space — all with full control and undo safety.

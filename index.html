CirroGuide

Your cloud cleanup home

CirroGuide helps you understand, clean, and optimize cloud storage across Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive.

Privacy policy Contact support

What CirroGuide does

  • Builds a storage summary across connected providers
  • Finds exact duplicate files and helps you reclaim space
  • Lets you preview files before cleanup
  • Tracks reclaimed space across your CirroGuide account

Supported providers

  • Google Drive
  • Dropbox
  • OneDrive

iCloud support is planned for a future update.

How CirroGuide handles data

What syncs

CirroGuide account profile details, connected-provider summaries, reclaimed-space totals, and lightweight dashboard metadata can be synced to support cross-device restore.

What stays local

Heavy duplicate scan state, preview files, temporary caches, and local provider sessions stay on your device unless explicitly reconnected.
